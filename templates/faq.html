Frequently Asked Questions

The required license depends on the class and tonnage of the vessel. Basic interplanetary permits are sufficient for smaller transports and exploration ships (Class 1-3). Larger freighters or combat-rated vessels may require advanced commercial or military-grade certifications. Please check the specific requirements listed on each ship's detail page or contact us for clarification.

Our standard minimum rental period is 24 standard hours. Maximum rental durations can vary but typically extend up to one standard year. For longer-term leases, please contact our corporate accounts division.

Basic hull and liability insurance meeting standard Galactic Trade Federation requirements is included in the rental fee. Optional enhanced coverage for cargo, high-risk zones, or specific mission parameters can be purchased separately during the booking process.

We accept major interstellar credit systems (Galactic Credits, Federation Dollars) and certified bank transfers from recognized financial institutions. Full payment or a secured deposit is typically required before vessel handover. Details are provided during booking.

All our vessels undergo rigorous maintenance. However, should you experience technical difficulties, 24/7 emergency support is available via subspace communication. Contact details and procedures are provided in your rental packet. Depending on the situation and your location, remote assistance or a dispatched repair crew may be provided.

Unauthorized modifications to the vessel's structure, systems, or software are strictly forbidden. Temporary installation of approved, non-invasive personal equipment may be permitted with prior written consent. Please discuss any specific needs with us beforehand.
